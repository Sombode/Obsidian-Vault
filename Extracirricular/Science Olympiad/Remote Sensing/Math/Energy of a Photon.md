$$Q = \frac{hc}{λ}$$
Where $Q$ is the energy of quantum in [[Joules]] (J),  
$h$ is [[Planck's Constant]] (6.626E-34 J sec),  
$c$ is the [[Speed of Light]] (299 792 458 m / s),
$λ$ is wavelength
